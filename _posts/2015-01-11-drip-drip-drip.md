Now I think that if I were to use drugs again,  
it would in fact be an anticlimax.  
It would be a surrender  
to the incessant drip, drip, drip  
of existence.  
現在我卻覺得如果再次吸毒  
會是一個非常掃興的狗血劇情  
只不過是屈服於  
持續的滴水狀態  滴答滴答地  
流失自我
